## 4. $50 Pro Ticket - 20 spots round 1

What it is:
- ZAOstock is free to attend. Always.
- Pro Ticket is $50, optional, supports the festival.
- Pro Ticket buyers get a 1:1 with someone on the ZAO team before the event.
- We use that 1:1 to find them a real way to plug in.
- Pro Ticket holders credited as supporters on the festival page.

Round 1 cap: 20 spots only.

Why a cap:
- Real scarcity drives action vs open-ended donation
- 20 x 1:1 commitments is the realistic upper bound for the team
- We raise the cap after round 1 closes if demand is there

Discussion points:
- Who should the 20 first-round Pro Ticket holders be? Existing ZAO members get priority?
- Who on the team can take 1:1 calls? Aim for 4-5 of us split across the 20.
- Payment routing - PayPal (zaalpanthaki) and Giveth. Confirmed.
- Tracking - manual for now, light spreadsheet, until we wire it up properly.
- Close round 1 when: 20 sold OR Sep 1 2026, whichever first.
