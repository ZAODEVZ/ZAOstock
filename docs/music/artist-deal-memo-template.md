## Travel + lodging

ZAOstock supports artist travel via the following channels (artist picks one, in order of preference):

1. **ZAO Festivals direct cover** - if event budget allows by August 15, ZAOstock covers travel + 1 night hotel directly. Decided on a case-by-case basis based on event budget at that point.
2. **Per-artist crowdfund pool** - ZAOstock helps artist run a Giveth or GoFundMe campaign for travel. Funds raised go directly to the artist. ZAOstock shares the campaign across our channels.
3. **Self-funded** - artist covers their own travel. ZAO Festivals provides hotel recommendations and local connections to reduce cost.

The path will be confirmed in writing by August 15, 2026.

## Compensation

ZAOstock operates at break-even. There is no guaranteed performance fee for Year 2 (2026). Artist compensation comes from:

- 100% of any merch sold by the artist on-site (ZAOstock does not take a cut)
- 100% of any tips collected for the artist
- Recording of the set, owned by the artist, free to use commercially
- Recap content + photography credit, owned mutually
- Crowdfund support per the travel section above

For Year 3 (2027) and beyond, ZAOstock targets a guaranteed performance fee structure. Year 2 is brand-building.

## Image + recording rights

- Artist retains full ownership of their performance recording.
- ZAOstock retains the right to use audio and video clips up to 90 seconds in length for promotional content (recap reel, social posts, future event marketing).
- For commercial use beyond promotion (e.g. paid streaming distribution), parties will negotiate separately.
- Photography taken at ZAOstock by ZAO Festivals photographers is shared with the artist for free use in their own promotion.

## Cancellation

- If ZAOstock cancels the event, the artist is released with no further obligation. Any travel costs already incurred by the artist (deposits, non-refundable bookings) are the responsibility of ZAOstock to refund up to a cap of $300 per artist.
- If the artist cancels with more than 30 days notice, no penalty.
- If the artist cancels with less than 30 days notice, ZAOstock will work with them in good faith to find a solution. Repeated late cancellations affect future bookings.
- Force majeure (weather, public health, etc.): both parties act in good faith. ZAOstock may move the event to a backup date or virtual format with artist's consent.

## Insurance + safety

- ZAOstock carries event liability insurance via the production partner (ENTERACT) and Fractured Atlas COI for the venue.
- The artist is responsible for their own gear insurance. ZAOstock provides reasonable security for the stage area but does not insure private equipment.
- If the artist requires a Certificate of Insurance for their own paperwork, request via email and we will arrange.

## How to use this template

1. Copy this file. Replace `[bracketed]` placeholders with real values.
2. Send as PDF or rich-text email body to the artist (and their manager, if applicable).
3. Save the signed version to `docs/music/deals/[artist-name]-2026.md` once confirmed.
4. Update the `artists` table on ZAO STOCK Supabase: set `confirmed = true`, paste deal terms in `notes`.

## What this template intentionally does NOT include

- Specific dollar fees (no guaranteed performance fee for Year 2)
- Exclusivity clauses (artist can play other festivals)
- Equity / token grants (out of scope - separate conversation if relevant)
- Long IP rights transfers (we do not own the artist's catalog)

If a specific deal needs any of those, escalate to Zaal + FailOften before sending.
