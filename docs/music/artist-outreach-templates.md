# Artist Outreach Templates - ZAOstock 2026

> **For:** Music circle members reaching out to potential ZAOstock artists.
> **Goal:** Land 5 confirmed artists by July 1.
> **Tone:** Direct, specific, respectful of the artist's time. ZAO-coded but not exclusionary.

## Template 1 - Cold DM (X / Instagram / Farcaster)

Use when: you have not interacted with the artist before but you know their work.

```
Hey [name] - Zaal here from The ZAO.

We are putting on our first IRL festival in Maine on October 3, 2026.
Outdoor, free to walk in, all-day, independent artists only. Targeting 200-400
in person + 1K+ via livestream.

Would love to have you on the lineup. Would your team be open to a 15-min
intro call this week?

If you want context first - zaostock.com/musicians has the full picture.

Either way, big fan of [reference one specific track / show / project]. Onward.
```

**Customization rules:**
- The reference at the end is mandatory. Generic "big fan" reads spammy. Name a specific track, a specific show, a specific moment.
- If you do not have time to find one specific reference, do not send the message yet.

## Template 2 - Warm referral DM

Use when: a mutual connection has introduced you, OR the artist has interacted with The ZAO community before.

```
Hey [name] - Zaal here. [Mutual] mentioned you might be open to playing
ZAOstock. Got a sec to talk October 3 in Ellsworth Maine?

Quick context: this is our third ZAO Festivals event after PALOOZA NYC
(April 2024) and CHELLA Miami (December 2024, 544 RSVPs). Same vibe -
independent artists, one stage, all day, free to attend.

zaostock.com/musicians has details. If interested, what is the best way
to send over a deal memo + travel info?
```

## Template 3 - Email (longer-form, for artists with managers / agents)

Use when: the artist has formal representation and you need a paper trail.

```
Subject: ZAOstock 2026 - October 3 - lineup invitation for [Artist Name]

[Manager / Booking Agent name] -

Reaching out to invite [Artist Name] to play the inaugural ZAOstock festival
on Friday October 3, 2026 in Ellsworth, Maine.

About the event:
ZAOstock is a one-day outdoor festival produced by ZAO Festivals - the
team behind PALOOZA NYC (April 2024) and CHELLA Miami (December 2024,
544 RSVPs). Free to attend, all-day, independent music only. Target
200-400 in person plus 1K+ via livestream.

What we offer:
- 25-minute set window (negotiable for headliners)
- Travel + lodging covered or supported via per-artist crowdfund pool
  (Giveth + GoFundMe)
- Standard recording + photography rights stay with the artist
- 100% of any merch / tip revenue stays with the artist
- Recording of the set for [Artist Name]'s own use

Production:
The festival operates at break-even. ZAO Festivals collaborates with
ENTERACT for production support. Eligible public funding is administered
through New Media Commons (a fiscally sponsored project of Fractured Atlas,
501(c)(3)) for sponsors who want tax-deductible recognition. We are happy
to share full production details + insurance / safety documentation as we
move forward.

Would [Artist Name] be open to a brief intro call to discuss? Happy to
work around their schedule.

zaostock.com/musicians has additional context.

Onward,
[Your name]
[ZAO Festivals / circle role]
```

## Template 4 - Follow-up (no response after 5-7 days)

Use when: first DM / email got no response.

```
Hey [name] - circling back on the ZAOstock invite. No worries if it is
not a fit for your schedule. If you want me to take you off the list,
just say so. Otherwise, October 3, free to attend, Maine, 25-min set.
Happy to chat whenever.
```

**Rules for follow-up:**
- Maximum ONE follow-up. Never two.
- If no response after follow-up, move on. Add to a "may revisit Year 3" list.

## Template 5 - Confirmation (after they say yes)

Use when: artist has verbally agreed to play.

```
Hey [name] - amazing. Locking [Artist Name] in for ZAOstock October 3.

Next steps on my side:
1. Sending the deal memo today (one page, simple - what we cover, what we
   ask, set time, technical rider intake form).
2. Your manager / agent gets a copy too if applicable.
3. Travel + lodging: [details based on what was discussed].

Next steps on your side:
1. Send the technical rider whenever ready (no rush, deadline is Aug 15).
2. Confirm headshot + 1-paragraph bio for the lineup announcement.
3. Hold the date - we will share the public announcement when 5 artists
   are confirmed.

Welcome to the lineup. Onward.
```

## Tone notes (apply to all templates)

- **No "literally" / "honestly" / "to be honest"** filler.
- **No emoji.** ZAO Festivals does not use them in formal copy.
- **No em dashes.** Use hyphens.
- **Reference one specific work** by the artist - prove you know who they are.
- **Lead with the value to them**, then explain the asks. Never the other way around.
- **Sign off with "Onward"** - that is our voice.

## What NOT to send

- Form letters with no personal reference
- Anything claiming "exclusive opportunity" or "limited slots"
- Long history of The ZAO before the actual ask
- Fee discussions in the first message (defer to call)
- Promises about specific attendance numbers we cannot back up

## Tracking

Every outreach should log to the `contact_log` table on ZAO STOCK Supabase via the bot or dashboard. Format:
- Artist name
- Channel (DM / email / referral)
- Template used (1-5 from above)
- Date sent
- Response (yes / no / no-response / pending)
- Owner (the music circle member who sent it)

This becomes the data behind sponsor + investor decks ("X outreach attempts, Y conversion rate, Z confirmed").
